Child Safety Standards

Avlodona is committed to the safety of every child. This page outlines our policies and enforcement procedures.

🛡️

Zero Tolerance Policy

Avlodona strictly prohibits any content that sexually exploits or abuses minors. We have absolute zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form. Violations result in immediate account termination and reporting to authorities.

🚫

Prohibited Content

  • Any sexual or sexualised content involving minors
  • Grooming, exploitation, or manipulation of minors
  • Any content, media, or communication that endangers child safety
  • Solicitation of personal information from minors for harmful purposes
  • Sharing, distributing, or promoting child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Any activity that facilitates trafficking or abuse of minors
📣

Reporting a Violation

Users can report child safety violations directly within the Avlodona app without leaving the platform. Report buttons are available on every profile, post, and message.

1
Tap the ⋯ menu or Report button Available on any profile, post, comment, or message.
2
Select "Child Safety" category Choose the most specific reason that describes the violation.
3
Submit your report Your report is sent immediately to our Trust & Safety team for urgent review.
⚖️

Enforcement & Compliance

  • All child safety reports are reviewed immediately upon receipt
  • Accounts confirmed to violate these standards are permanently banned
  • All confirmed CSAM violations are reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant regional authorities
  • Avlodona cooperates fully with law enforcement investigations
  • Our platform complies with applicable child safety laws and regulations
